Notes for: John Vance KELL


John Vance KELL moved to Gibson co, IN, with his parents in 1827
from South Carolina. They settled a mile southwest of Princeton.
Six years later, they moved to Wabash Co, IL, where the elder
KELL died. They family returned to Gibson Co, where Mrs. KELL
died. John KELL was one of the well-to-do farmers in Gibson Co.

Eunice Kelley said that John KELL was turned down for military
service because of one crossed eye.

1850 Census of Gibson Co, IN. John V. KELL (age 23, SC, $500 real estate)
and Jane M. (age 29, Ten). Both married within the past year.

1870 Census of Gibson Co, IN. John V. KELL (age 42, SC), Jane (age 49),
Margaretta (age 20, IN), Mary(age 18, IN), Elizabeth A. (age 16, IN),
James C (age 12, IN), William T (age 10, IN), and John M (age 8, IN).
