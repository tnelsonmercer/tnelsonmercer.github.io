Notes for: James R NELSON

1)James R. Nelson first appears in the 1842 tax lists of Marion Co, KY, with one
child 7-17 years old.
2)Marion Co, KY, was part of Washington Co, prior to 1834.
3)James R. Nelson was twice widowed by the time of his marriage to
Nancy SCANLAND,according to their marriage record.
4) Gary Winters claims that the "R." stands for Robert. However, in "Our People
of Pike Co, IN" by Ruth Miley McClellan, his middle name is given as "Rote".
5) James and wife Sarah are listed in 1850 census of Marion Co, KY.
6) Last year that James R. is listed in tax lists of Marion Co, KY, is 1854.
7) James R. shows up in Marion Twp, Pike Co, IN, census in 1860 with wife Nancy,
children John, Sarah M, Perry C, Amanda F., and James C. Scanlin(blacksmith) and
Robert F. Scanlin, presumably brothers of Nancy. James C. Scanland came from
Marion Co, KY, in Aug. 1858, according to "Pike Co, IN, History" by McClellan. He
was the first postmaster of Otwell.
