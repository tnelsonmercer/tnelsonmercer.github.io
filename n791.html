Notes for: David FARIS

Martha Meyer notes;
Mrs John farris notes;

Will of Robert Farris, will book A, page 130, case no 56, file no 147;

David and Nancy were in 1810 census of Livingston Co, KY, along with his mother, Hannah.
David and Nancy came to Indiana around 1817, but can't locate in 1820 Indiana census.
His children born 1817-1820 say they were born in Indiana.
David and Nancy were in 1830 census of Gibson Co, IN.
