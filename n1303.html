Notes for: James Houston NELSON

James H. NELSON is in the Marion Co, KY, tax list for the following years: 1840-1844, 1847, 1849, and 1850.
No land value is ever listed.

James H. (age 32, b KY) and Frances(age 24, b KY) appear in 1850 census of Marion Co, KY, along with
son Gabriel H. (age 10, b KY), John W. (age 4, b KY), and Louisa J. (age 2, b KY).

Based Frances' young age of ~15 when Gabriel was born, it is possible that Gabriel was a son of an earlier marriage. However, with Frances' father's name also being Gabriel, he probably is the son of Frances.

James H. (age 43, b KY) and Francis (age 34, b KY) appear in 1860 census of Marion Twp, Pike Co, IN,
with son Gabriel (age 18, b KY), John W. (age 14, b MO), Eliza J. (age 12, b KY), James B. (age 9, b KY),
Frederick M. (age 7, b IN), Redford M. (age 5, b IN), and George W. (age 2, b IN).

From the age and place of birth of children in the 1860 census, James and Frances came to Pike Co
from Marion Co, KY, between 1851 and 1853.

About 1854, "Gabe" Nelson discovered the Sweet Sulpher Springs on his fathers farm about two miles
north of Velpen. These springs were later developed into a health resort in the latter part of the 1800s.
"Pike Co, IN, History", McClellan.

James H. (age 54, b KY) and Frances (age 44?, b KY) appear in 1870 census of Marion Twp, Pike Co,
IN, with Fredrick Mouser (age 18), Redford (age 14), George Washington (age 11), Thomas (age 10),
Arthur (age 10), and Sallie (age 1). All children listed born in IN.

James H. (age 63, b KY,VA,VA) and Francis (age 55, b KY,KY,KY) appear in 1880 census of Marion
Twp, Pike Co, IN, with James B. (age 26, b KY,KY,KY), George (age 21, b IN,KY,KY), Thomas(age 18,
b IN,KY,KY) and Author (age 14, b IN,KY,KY).

Will witnessed and signed 8 June, 1880, in Pike Co, IN. Lists wife Frances Nelson, of Marion
Twp, and children Gabriel H. Nelson, John W. Nelson (deceased?), Louisa J. Hume, James
B. Nelson, Frederick M. Nelson, Redford M. Nelson, Geroge W. Nelson, Thomas L. Nelson,
and Arthur Mc. Nelson. Will probated 24 July 1880.

Tombstone at Flat Creek Cem. lists death date as 15 July 1880, 63 yr, 7 mo, 18 days.
