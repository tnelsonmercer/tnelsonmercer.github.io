Notes for: George Paul Torrance HUTCHINSON

Lived in Union Co, Illinois; Hamilton Co, Il; Sullivan Co, In; Gibson Co, In; Pike
Co, In; He moved to Pike Co, IN, when the Wabash-Erie Canal was being
built. He was a farmer;
According to Fred Hutchinson, George lived north-east of Spurgeon.
re: Barbara Euler notes; 1870-1880 Census Gibson Co, In; 1860 Pike Co, In
Census.
