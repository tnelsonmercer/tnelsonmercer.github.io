Notes for: Clara Ruddle

!The Riddell, Riddle, Ruddle Trail Vol V, No 2-3 Apr 1985-June 1986 made note
that all children of this marriage, their first mate's names and their mother
were cited in a suit Hawkins vs Byrd--O>S> 267; N>S> 94--Bill, 1813 (in Records
of Augusta Co, VA ,II, p 202); and it was stated that they all were living in
Virginia at that date, 1813. The suit was over land in Shenandoah Co. Smutz,
p 5, gave the marriage dates but did not indicate the place of marriage; nor
where the license was issued. The compiler found the licenses cited above in
SHENDANDOAH CO, VA MARRIAGES, 1772-1850. The others were not recorded there.
They probably lived on Smith Creek, in Dunmore (now Shenandoah) Co, Va, and
probably all the listed children were born there and many of them continued to
live in that area.
