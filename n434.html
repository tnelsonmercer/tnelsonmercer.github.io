Joseph Zunhammer, stove mounter 1823 E. Oregon.

Mary I Zunhammer.
Vanderberg Co, In Health Dept. records on childrens births and deaths
Marriage records Vanderberg Co, In for children lists Joseph Zunhammer, father
born Germany, residence, Wadesville, In, and mother Ida Jenkins.

Fulton Ave. Have a picture of a Mary Zunhammer.
Zunhammers disappear from City
Directory 1907 and Can't find them in Posey County, no trace again after 1922
when son Joseph married.
