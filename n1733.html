Notes for: Andrew ANDERSON

"History of Pike and Dubois Co, IN" page 449, says that Andrew came to
Indiana in 1816 from Mercer Co, KY.

Account of George R. Wilson says that Andrew and brother William, Jr,
came to Dubois Co in 1814 and built a log cabin. The rest of the family
followed and reached Dubois Co on May 7, 1815.

Commissioned a Lieutenant in the Indiana State Militia June 20, 1823.

1850 Census of Benbridge Twp, Dubois Co, IN:
Andrew Anderson (age 54, farmer, 1200, KY), Obedience (age 48, KY),
William (age 16, farmer, IN, school), and Edward WILLIAMS (age 6, IN).

1860 Census of Benbridge Twp, Dubois Co, IN:
Andrew Anderson (age 64, farmer 2350, 1000, KY), Obediant (age 49),
George (age 9) and Selina (age 6).
