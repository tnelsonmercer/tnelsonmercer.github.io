Notes for: Avis Ann JENKINS

!Liked to sew and quilt. made quilts for all of her children and grandchildren.
went to school a while at Mitchell School west of Aunt Julie's house in
country south of Francisco, Ind. Then moved to Somerville. Lived for a while
with Grandpa Hutchinson in old house where Aunt Julie lived (narrated to Liz
Nelson by Avis Sharp)
