Notes for: Beulah WILLIAMS

Age 76, of Mackey, died at 4:45 a.m. at St Mary's Medical Center in
Evansville, IN. Member of Clark's Chapel General Baptist Church, Community
Club, Birthday Club, and providence Ladies Aid. Surviving are three daughters
and one son, 17 grandchildren, 20 great-grandchildren. Funeral services were
at Providence Church. Burial Providence Cemetery near Somerville. Corn-Colvin
Funeral Home in Oakland City was in charge of arrangements.

A contradicting obiturary listed burial at Clark Chapel Cem.
