Notes for: James R SKELTON


1860 Census of Gibson Co, IN: Jas. R. Skelton (age 34, farmer, 600,500,IN,
can't read/write), Mary J. (age 34, IN, can't read/write), Mary C. (age 12, IN),
Sarah E. (age 11, IN), Joel H. (age 8, IN), John (H?) (age 2, IN), Henry (C?)
(2m, IN).

1870 Census of Columbia Twp, Gibson Co, IN: James R. Skelton (age 44,
Farmer, 4800,1200,IN,no read/write), Mary J. (age 43, no read/write), Joel H.
(age 18), Henry (age 11), Jasper E. (age 7), Margaret (age 4), Dicey E.
Loyd (age 22, domestic servant).

The death certificate of James R. says that he was single and does not list a
spouse, although he died ten years before Mary Jane.
